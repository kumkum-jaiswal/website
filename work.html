ABOUT THE SHOP

I’m a text box. Double click me to edit the text or to change the way I look. You can change the font, size, color, and so much more. For advanced styling options, click on “more” located on the right hand side of the text editing menu. Click once to move me or to bring up the resize handles and Squeegee Tool. The resize handles allow you to make me any size you want.
